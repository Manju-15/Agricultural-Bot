About Us

Farm AI-BOT is a conversational virtual assistants which provides automate interactions with end users. It is a Artificial Intelligence powered ChatBot using Machine Learning techniques which understand natural language and interact with users in a personalized way. Our Farm AI-BOT could also leverage the emerging agriculture technologies by assisting farmers with answers to their questions, providing advice and recommendations on farming related problems using advances agritechnologies and methods. Our Farm AI-BOT conversational assistant uses Natural Language Processing techniques to understand the user queries in their natural language. This will make the system understand even the grammatically not well defined sentences as input queries. It also answers all the basic queries of a farmers and can also provide a accurate solutions to all queries of farmers. Our FarmAI-BOT ChatBot has been created to specifically address the needs of Indian farmers. The information was gathered from farmer discussions at Kisan Call Centers (KCC). Our FarmAI-BOT is based on the idea of assisting farmers by providing them with latest agritechnologies.
